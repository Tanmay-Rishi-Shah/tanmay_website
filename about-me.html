Greetings, I'm Tanmay Rishi Shah, an 11-year-old with a zest for life and a deep passion for acting. You may recognize me from some of your favorite TV shows like "Yeh Rishta Kya Kehlata Hai" and "Ghum Hai Kisi Ke Pyar Mein" on Star Plus, as well as "Kammna" on Sony TV.

Acting isn't just a career for me; it's a way to express myself and connect with audiences. Whether I'm portraying a character's emotions or delivering impactful dialogues, I pour my heart and soul into every role, striving to create memorable experiences for viewers.

When I'm not in front of the camera, I lead an active lifestyle filled with sports and intellectual pursuits. I'm an avid basketball player, finding joy in the fast-paced action and team camaraderie on the court. Badminton is another favorite, offering a blend of strategy and skill that keeps me engaged and focused.

Skating is a passion that allows me to glide effortlessly, feeling the wind in my hair as I explore new tricks and moves. Cricket is a sport that runs in my veins, both as a player and a fan, instilling in me a love for the game's strategic depth and excitement.
Swimming is not just a recreational activity for me; it's a form of relaxation and a way to stay fit. The water is where I feel free and energized, ready to take on new challenges both in and out of the pool.

Beyond sports, I find solace in the world of literature. Reading books is not just a hobby; it's a journey into new worlds, ideas, and perspectives. I'm particularly drawn to stories that inspire and challenge me, sparking my imagination and expanding my horizons.

Solving math equations is another passion of mine. I enjoy the logical reasoning and problem-solving skills it requires, constantly seeking new challenges to test my mathematical prowess.
Join me as I navigate the exciting and dynamic world of acting, sports, and learning. Thank you for being a part of my journey!
